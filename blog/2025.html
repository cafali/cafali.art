SkinDealz 1.5.9 is here!

v1.5.9 introduces improvements to the InCart system, making it easier to reserve items when adding them manually. You no longer need to hold SHIFT on the last selected item—simply double-tap SHIFT to be redirected to the cart page, where the reservation process starts automatically. Shortcut adjustments include changing Instant Scroll Up from SHIFT+T to T only and making double-pressing SHIFT a quick way to access the cart page. Additionally, the grabber.js config sequence has been adjusted to AMOUNT - PERCENT - ITEM for better organization, and the About Page has been updated.
