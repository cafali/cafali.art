CherryPad 1.6.1 is here!

Fixed a bug where red spellcheck underlines appeared through the Privacy layer when Privacy Mode was on. Added visual feedback to note areas: the active area darkens when focused and becomes slightly brighter on hover. Added an easily accessible star review system on the About page that links directly to the Chrome Web Store review page.

CherryPad 1.6.0 is here!

1.5.x is now history. This update brings small improvements to features introduced in the previous release, mainly the privacy mode, while also improving usability. Popup buttons now include icons, and the Clear button bug that prevented the note field from being cleared when triggered from the popup has been fixed; the Clear button is now also paired with a two-step confirmation to prevent accidental note deletion. The CherryPad logo now fades when overlapping buttons during horizontal fullscreen resizing, and the redirect method on the About page was adjusted to better support multiple elements.

CherryPad 1.5.9 is here!

This update introduces a new privacy toggle in fullscreen mode, allowing users to quickly hide the contents of their notes and reveal them again with a click or hover. Notifications for save, copy, download, and paste actions now include the current date and time, with colors matching the action buttons for clearer feedback. The update also refreshes the about page, adds a small hint message to the hidden minigame, and introduces a subtle animated dark gradient background in fullscreen mode. General code cleanup and optimization were also performed to improve overall performance.
