CherryPad

From curiosity about how browser plugins work and

how to build them to a full-blown notepad extension.


While designing CherryPad, my goal was

to create an eye-catching and eye-pleasing

minimalistic experience with various features

and an easy-to-use and intuative UI.


This is my first ever extension that went

through multiple updates, quality fixes, and more.
