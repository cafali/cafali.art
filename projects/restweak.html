ResTweak

I created this to quickly switch to 4:3

when playing CS. This allows me to play

4:3 in fullscreen windowed mode, where

I don't need to worry about the long

alt+tabbing issue that occurs in fullscreen

mode and often bugs out when playing 4:3.


It automatically toggles between the resolutions

depending on your current setting.
