SkinDealz

SkinDealz is a browser extension designed to

automate and speed up purchases in gaming

marketplaces, especially on the LIVE market.


By holding the SHIFT key while adding

items to the cart, it streamlines the buying

process, completing transactions in under 2900ms.


It streamlines and speeds up the buying process,

giving users a competitive advantage in securing items.
