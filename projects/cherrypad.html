What is CherryPad?

CherryPad is a sleek and modern notepad extension built for effortless note-taking directly inside your browser. With its dark themed interface it stays easy on the eyes during long writing sessions. You can jot down quick ideas from the popup or switch to fullscreen mode for a larger workspace with two note fields. Notes can be saved directly inside the extension or exported as .txt files whenever you need them.
